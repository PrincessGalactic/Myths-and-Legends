Greek Mythology

When you think of Greek Mythology, you probably think of Greek Gods, or if you are
like me, you will think of Percy Jackson (mainly the book series written by Rick Riordon).
However, it isn't simply just Gods and Goddesses, there are so many more creatures and stories
that refer to Greek Mythology.
On this page, I will be listing all the Greek Gods and Goddesses, along with all the creatures
and stories that come with Greek Mythology.

Greek Gods

  • Zeus; The God of the Sky
  • Poseidon; The God of the Sea
  • Hades; The God of the Underworld
  • Ares; The God of War
  • Apollo; The God of Archery, Music, Poetry, Prophecy and the Sun
  • Hephaestus; The God of Blacksmiths and Fire
  • Hermes; The God of Roadways, Travelers, Merchants and Thieves
  • Dionysus; The God of Wine
  • Hypnos; The God of Sleep
  • Janus; The God of Beginnings, Choices and Doorways

Greek Goddesses

  • Hera; The Goddess of Marriage, Mothers and Family
  • Demeter; The Goddess of Agriculture
  • Athena; The Goddess of Wisdom, War and Useful Arts
  • Artemis; The Goddess of the Moon, the Hunt and Young Maidens
  • Aphrodite; The Goddess of Love and Beauty
  • Nike; The Goddess of Victory
  • Nemisis; The Goddess of Revenge
  • Iris; The Goddess of the Rainbow, Messenger of the Gods
  • Hecate; The Goddess of Magic, Crossroads and Ghosts
  • Tyche; The Goddess of Good Luck and Fortune
